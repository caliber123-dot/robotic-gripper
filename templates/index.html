Spring Structural Model of Robotic Gripper

According to this equation:

m = mass of the object
g = acceleration due to gravity = 9.8 m/s²
r = distance of finger from joint to contact point
θ = angle of contact
k = spring constant
dθ/dt = first derivative
d²θ/dt² = second derivative
r sinθ = linear deflection
(θ - θ³/3! + θ⁵/5! + ...) = Taylor series expansion of sin
Fᵢ(t) = Grasp Force
